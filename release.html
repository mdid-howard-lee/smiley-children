Eighteen months of writing toward the same dinner table. Smiley Children opens its front door on October 23, 2026.

Development
timeline.

From a twelve-page dinner scene to a seven-chapter game — every milestone Hollow Window Studio hit along the way, and the ones still ahead.

  1. April 2024

    Story seed

    First draft of the script. A single dinner-table scene, twelve pages long. The line "I'm not asking you to be perfect" exists from day one.

    Complete
  2. August 2024

    Greenlight

    Hollow Window Studio formally commits. Three writers, two illustrators, one composer, and a part-time family therapist on retainer.

    Complete
  3. December 2024

    Vertical slice

    Playable prototype of the first chapter. The dinner argument works. The Smiley Coins system gets named for the first time, scribbled on a napkin.

    Complete
  4. May 2025

    Cast art locked

    Final designs for the son, the daughter, the parents, and the best friend. Eight months of revisions. The son's hoodie went through fourteen shades of green.

    Complete
  5. September 2025

    Alpha build

    All seven chapters playable end-to-end. The first time someone outside the studio cried at the ending. Mira's path takes 6 hours; the son's takes 7.

    Complete
  6. February 2026

    Closed beta

    One hundred testers across three continents. Two endings get rewritten — including the one we thought was the best.

    Complete
  7. May 2026 — Now

    Final polish

    Voice direction, localization to five languages, accessibility passes. Every scene, again. The composer is on his sixth re-edit of the credits theme.

    In progress
  8. August 2026

    Free demo

    The first two chapters, free for everyone. Two hours of playtime. No spoilers. One Smiley Coin to take home with you.

    Upcoming
  9. October 23, 2026

    Launch

    Smiley Children goes live worldwide. PC · Mac · Console · Steam Deck verified. Seven chapters, seven endings, one family.
