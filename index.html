01 About the Game
Estimated playtime 8 — 12 hrs

Improve the family.
Earn the right
to be heard.

Between Expectations is a roleplay simulation about a teenager with a dream their parents refuse to accept. You play as either the son — who wants to be a singer — or the daughter — who wants to be an artist. Only one. The other lives a story you'll never see.

Across one autumn semester, you'll rebuild trust in a household that's forgotten how to listen. Bank Smiley Coins for every small kindness. Switch between first and third person at will. Take on side quests. And when you finally raise the subject of your dream, your parents will remember everything you've done.

A family,

Across one autumn semester, the dinner table becomes a battlefield no one knows they're fighting. The dream is small. It's just a guitar, or just a sketchbook. But this house has never been good at small things.
