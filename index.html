01 About the Game

A story about the words
we never say out loud.

Between Expectations follows a teenager caught between who their parents want them to become and the dream they're afraid to name. Every dinner, every text, every silence becomes a decision.

A narrative roleplay simulation about communication, trust, and the long quiet weight of family. Speak honestly, lie to keep the peace, or say nothing at all — your dialogue choices reshape relationships and lead to one of seven possible endings.
