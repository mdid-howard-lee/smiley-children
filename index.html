01 About the Game
Estimated playtime 8 — 12 hrs

A story about the words
we never say out loud.

Between Expectations follows a teenager caught between who their parents want them to become and the dream they're afraid to name. Every dinner, every text, every silence becomes a decision.

A narrative roleplay simulation about communication, trust, and the long quiet weight of family. Speak honestly, lie to keep the peace, or say nothing at all — your dialogue choices reshape relationships and lead to one of seven possible endings.

03 The Story

A family,
in a country of small rooms.

Across one autumn semester, the dinner table becomes a battlefield no one knows they're fighting. The dream is small. It's just a guitar, or just a sketchbook. But this house has never been good at small things.

Theme 01

Academic Pressure

When grades become love letters, every red mark is a rejection.

Theme 02

Misunderstanding

Two people in the same room, speaking two different languages of care.

Theme 03

Emotional Distance

A door closed for so long that no one remembers who shut it first.
