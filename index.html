01 About the Game
Playtime A semester. Or just an evening.
PLEASANT HOUSE, UNPLEASANT HOME

Please your

lose yourself.

Smiley Children is a roleplay simulation about a teenager with a dream their parents refuse to accept. Play as the son who wants to sing, or the daughter who wants to draw — only one. The other lives a story you'll never see.

You'll fight for your dream while maintaining a healthy relationship with the people you love. Every choice shifts the balance. Every small kindness fills your pocket with Smiley Coins — the currency of warmth, the price of being heard.

  • Choose son or daughter — singer or artist
  • Improve family bonds, earn Smiley Coins
  • Side quests, switchable POV, dialogue choices
  • Multiple endings shaped by trust and timing

05 Location

Pleasant house,
unpleasant home.

Warm lamps. Family photos. A dining table set for four. Everything looks like a place you'd want to live — until you live there. Across one apartment, one school, and the long walk between them, the silences pile up like furniture nobody moves.
